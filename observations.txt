*It is best to get into the habit of writing your folder and file names lowercase with no spaces 
 and with words separated by dashes.

*To rename a directory <dir> to <dir-renamed> use the command <mv dir dir-renamed>
It's not necessary to create <dir-renamed> before use <mv> command.

*How to open devtools in browser:
-> Ctrl+Shift+I
-> Internet Explorer and Edge: F12
